English Muffin Pizzas

You love pizza, and you love saving time. This timeless pizza recipe brings your two loves together.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of six English muffins
  • 1 can of Prego Traditional pasta sauce
  • 1 small block of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Place halved English muffins cut-side up on a baking sheet
  3. Spoon 1 to 2 tablespoons of pizza sauce onto each English muffin
  4. Use a shredder to cut fresh mozzarella to sprinkle on top of sauced muffins
  5. Place tray in the preheated oven until golden spots appear on the pizza's cheese, about 12 minutes
  6. Take cooked pizzas out of the oven and cool for 3-5 minutes before your first bite
