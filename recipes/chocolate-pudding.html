Chocolate Pudding

It's sweet. It's slimy. It's the perfect dessertâ€”with or without teeth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 box of My-T-Fine Chocolate Pudding mix

Steps

  1. Pour 2 cups of milk in a pot
  2. Set pot on the stove and set the burner to MED
  3. Pour in chocolate pudding mix and stir mixture continually in milk
  4. Once mixture begins to bubble and congeal, lower burner heat to LOW/SIMMER and continue stirring until pudding has a thick texture
  5. Pour cooked pudding into single serve glasses, cover dishes with plastic/foil wrap, and place dishes in refrigerator to cool for 2-3 hours
