Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Bagels

Get any day of the week going with this timeless grease bomb of protein, dairy, and carbs.

Ingredients

  • 6 strips of uncooked Oscar Myer bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 Thomas bagels
  • 2 slices of American cheese

Steps

  1. Cook strips of bacon in a frying pan until they a chewy with a light crisp
  2. Remove bacon from pan onto a plate lined with paper towels to absorb the grease
  3. Mix the whites and yolks of three eggs in a small measuring cup and then cook egg mixture in the pan's grease, stirring while it cooks
  4. Divide cooked eggs into two portions that share a simliar diameter to the bagel
  5. Cut two bagels in half and toast to a light brown
  6. Place a cooked egg portion on each of the bottom halves of toasted bagels
  7. Place one slice of cheese on each of the top halves of toasted bagels
  8. Break three slices of bacon into smaller pieces that can be balanced on top of the cooked eggs for each bagel
  9. Place top half of each bagel on top of the sandwich, letting the cheese melt atop the bacon and egg
